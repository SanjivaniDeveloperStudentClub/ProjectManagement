Welcome to Sanjivani's cutting-edge application designed to foster innovation, collaboration, and excellence within our academic community.

Our Mission:

At Sanjivani, we believe that some of the most groundbreaking ideas come from our very own students. To harness this creativity and drive positive change, we've developed an intuitive and powerful application that allows both students and staff to seamlessly manage project ideas and transform them into reality.

Who we are:

Streamlined Idea Submission: Students can easily submit their project ideas through our user-friendly platform, providing comprehensive details about their vision. Efficient Evaluation: Our staff members use robust criteria to evaluate project proposals, ensuring that each idea is given the attention it deserves. Collaboration: We encourage students to collaborate with peers, creating teams that can collectively enhance the impact of their projects. Transparent Tracking: Users can monitor the progress of their project ideas, receive feedback, and stay informed about the status of their proposals.
